Ba Sing Se


Like a cabbage, this city has layers. Just trying to enter the city was a whirlwind... I wanted to enter by ferry, but the line was so long I could feel myself wilting along with my cabbages. Then they tell me they won't let me board in case I have cabbage slugs! What even are cabbage slugs? Some weird hybrid of snail slugs and slug worms?

Once I got into the city I couldn't believe my eyes though. The modernity of the city! Perhaps I could learn a thing from these city folks and start my own business...
